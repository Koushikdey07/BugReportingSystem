About Us

A bug reporting system is a software application that is designed to help programmers to keep track of reported software bugs in their work. A major component of bug reporting system is a database that records facts and known bugs submitted by the tester. Bug Reporting System is an ideal solution to track the bugs of a product, solution or an application. Bug Reporting System allows individual or groups of developers to keep track of outstanding bugs in their product effectively.
